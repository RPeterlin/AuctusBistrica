Our Origin Story

It began in Kamnik on May 28, 2025 - a small town with a long history, and the place where a new one was about to be written. From the outset, Auctus Bistrica was built on a single, unwavering goal: to create an investment institution that would endure for generations, shaping the cities we live in, the industries we depend on, and the communities we call home.

What started with a close view of emerging real estate markets soon reached far beyond local borders. The vision expanded - from understanding the potential of new city districts to embracing the growth of transformative businesses. Today, that vision combines the stability of large-scale developments with the dynamic potential of companies poised to define the future.

We think in decades, not quarters. Our horizon stretches a century ahead, guided by a responsibility to preserve and grow capital for those who entrust it to us. We operate quietly, choosing each opportunity with precision. And we measure our success not only in returns, but in the lasting mark we leave on the world - a legacy to be inherited by the generations yet to come.

Our Focus

Investing in companies

We invest in early- and growth-stage companies with strong market potential, providing capital and guidance in exchange for equity.

Investing in real estate

We invest in large-scale real estate developments in emerging urban areas, concentrating on projects that help shape new city districts and support urban growth.

Invitation-only fund

As a closed, invitation-only fund, we work exclusively with a select group of partners, prioritizing long-term performance and capital preservation.
